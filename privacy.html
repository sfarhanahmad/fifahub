Privacy Policy

Overview

FIFA Hub 2026 ("this site," "we," "us") is an independent fan reference for the 2026 FIFA World Cup. This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to FIFA. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit, and how it's used.

Information We Collect

Local browser storage: The Predictions page saves your champion pick using your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Chat assistant: If you use the "Ask The Pundit" chat widget, your questions are sent to a third-party AI service (Groq) to generate a response. We do not store or review these conversations.

Cookies & Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use partner sites at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or aboutads.info/choices.

Analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g., which pages are popular). This data is anonymized and not used to identify individual visitors.

Third-Party Links

This site may link to external sites (e.g., official FIFA pages, news sources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via the contact details on our About page.
